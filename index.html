Portfolio

Avenue

A unified platform to help property managers oversee large scale developments and consolidate owners' properties in a secured portal. The app consists of a dynamic dashboard, geo-mapping and embedded digital signature form to aid managers in their daily operational tasks. It was initially a solo project and later worked in pairs to finalise the app.

A global climate hackathon organised by DEV and New Relic. I was part of a 5-person team who participated and won runner up. The app was developed to create awareness and actionable steps towards combating deforestation and help the earth recover to a time when trees were abundant.

A full stack app to ease administrative workload on school teachers and administrators. The feature added was an attendance tracker that shows a list of teachers, their assigned subjects and students being taught. The attendance tracker allows teachers to focus more time on teaching.
